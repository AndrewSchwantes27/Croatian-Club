Heritage Hall

Overview
Host your next landmark event in the magnificent Heritage Hall, the premier function space at the Croatian Community Centre Brisbane. Designed for grand-scale gatherings, this expansive hall combines classic elegance with modern functionality, providing the perfect canvas for your wedding, gala, corporate function, or major celebration.

Located on the first floor, the Heritage Hall makes a grand impression from the moment your guests arrive. Ascend one of two grand staircases or use the convenient lift access to enter a spectacular room designed to accommodate up to 800 guests. Its impressive size, large stage, and soaring ceilings create an atmosphere of sophistication and boundless potential.

We believe your event should be a true reflection of your style. The Heritage Hall offers unparalleled freedom to bring your vision to life.

  • Self-Catering Welcome: Take full control of your menu with our self-catering option.
  • No Noise Restrictions: Celebrate without limits. Our unique location means your event can continue at any hour, for any duration, without noise constraints.
  • Decorate to Your Desire: The hall is a blank canvas for you to transform. Tables and chairs are available on-site, ready for your unique decorative touch.
  • Ample On-Site Parking: Enjoy the convenience of a 300-space car park, included in your venue hire costs.

Create an event that will be remembered for years to come in a venue that offers both scale and flexibility.
