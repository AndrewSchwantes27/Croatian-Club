Community Centre Brisbane

Our Story: A Home Away From Home

The Croatian Community Centre Brisbane is more than just a building; it is the heart of the Croatian community in Brisbane and across Queensland. For generations, it has served as a cherished gathering place, a living link to our beloved homeland, and a testament to the enduring spirit of the Croatian people who first settled here.

For over half a century, the Croatian Club Brisbane has been a gathering place for community, culture, and celebration.

Our Beginnings

Between the 1940s and the 1990s, thousands of Croatians made the courageous journey to Australia, seeking hope, safety, and opportunity far from the turmoil and oppression that shadowed their homeland. With heavy hearts but unwavering determination, they embarked on a new life on distant shoresâbringing with them their traditions, their language, and their dreams for a brighter future. Australia opened its arms and became not just a refuge, but also a land where their own stories would take root. Over time, these settlers built more than homes; they wove the Croatian spirit into the rich tapestry of Australian society, and Australia itself became a treasured part of who they were.

A New Home

Our story began with these early Croatian immigrants, who carried with them the rich traditions, vibrant culture, and resilient spirit of their homeland. With courage and hard work, they planted new roots in Australian soil. But their hearts remained connected to the Adriatic shores, the Dinaric Alps, and the Pannonian plains they left behind. From this deep longing to preserve their heritage, the idea for our community centre was born. Carrying the Flame Forward

Today, as the generations born in Australia continue this journey, our story evolves. The centre stands as a proud symbol of the legacy of those first arrivals, and now bridges the past with the present by connecting younger generations to the land of their parents and grandparents.
